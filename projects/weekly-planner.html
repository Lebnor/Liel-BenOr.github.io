Weekly Planner

Ever feel like chrome's default new tab page is bland and uninspiring?

Weekly-planner replaces the new tab page and offers you a tool to quickly write your plans for the next week.

At the top we have quick access to frequent tabs. Tabs that are a part of educational content / for university are seperated.

At the bottom there is a short text field for each day of the week, so you can jot down important tasks for that day.
The current day is accentuated with gold borders.

In the settings for this extension you can change the color of the background and choose wether it should be gradient or not

I've personally used weekly-planner so I wouldn't have an excuse to forget to check my calendar...
